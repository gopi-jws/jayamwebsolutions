Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web design and development services.

How long does it take to build a website? +

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex websites or e-commerce platforms can take 8-12 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What information do you need to get started? +

To get started, we need information about your business, target audience, project goals, design preferences, and any specific functionality requirements. If you have existing brand assets like logos, color schemes, and content, those are helpful too. We'll guide you through this process during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance services? +

Yes, we offer website maintenance services to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, performance optimization, and technical support. We can tailor a maintenance plan to suit your specific needs.

Can you help with content creation? +

Yes, we offer content creation services including copywriting, image sourcing, and basic video production. Our content team works closely with you to create compelling, SEO-friendly content that engages your audience and communicates your brand message effectively.

Will my website be mobile-friendly? +

All websites we build are fully responsive and mobile-friendly by default. We test our websites on various devices and screen sizes to ensure they look and function perfectly regardless of how users access them. With mobile traffic accounting for over 50% of web traffic, mobile optimization is essential for any modern website.

Do you provide hosting services? +

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions for websites of all sizes. Our hosting packages include regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support. We can also help you set up hosting with a third-party provider if you prefer. We'll recommend the best hosting solution based on your website's requirements and expected traffic.
